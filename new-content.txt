Coaching Overview Section
Learn, Improve, and Play with Confidence
At Its Padel, our coaching programmes are designed to help players develop their skills, build confidence, and enjoy the excitement of padel. Whether you are stepping onto the court for the first time or looking to refine your competitive game, our experienced coaching team provides structured training in a welcoming environment.
Our academy offers programmes for adults, juniors, and beginners, with sessions focused on technique, strategy, and match play. Every session is designed to help players improve step by step while enjoying the social and energetic nature of padel.

Meet the Coach
Mark Openshaw
Mark Openshaw is the Head Coach at Its Padel and a Team GB player with extensive experience in racquet sports and professional coaching.
Mark is passionate about helping players improve their skills while building confidence and enjoying the sport. His coaching focuses on technique, tactical awareness, and match performance, ensuring players continue to develop at every level.
Through structured training sessions and personalised guidance, Mark helps players unlock their potential and fully experience the excitement of padel.
